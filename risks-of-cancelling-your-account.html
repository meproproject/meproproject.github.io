RISKS OF CANCELLING YOUR ACCOUNT

1. Invalid login

After cancelling your account, you will no longer be able to access the MePro app with the cancelled account.

2. Deleted data cannot be recovered

Following account cancellation, all MePro-related information, including educational records, photographs, and personal information, will be permanently and irreversibly deleted.

3. Assets and offers

- After cancelling, all MePro assets and offers will be deleted, including the number of VIP days. Valid vouchers will not be used for future transactions and after-sales processes.

- Customers who have purchased the course please contact the customer service department to cancel their account.
